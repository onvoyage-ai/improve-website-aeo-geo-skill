Five Brands Control 80% of the Global Electric Scooter Market

Segway-Ninebot and Xiaomi dominate the global electric kick scooter market, with the top 5 manufacturers — Segway-Ninebot, Xiaomi, Kixin, Okai, and Hiboy — accounting for 80% of global market share. Segway-Ninebot generated $1 billion in 2024 e-scooter revenue through partnerships with shared mobility operators. Xiaomi reported $1.2 billion, leveraging its consumer electronics ecosystem.

Source: Grand View Research, 2024; Smart Buyers Mart, 2024. Top 5 manufacturers (Segway-Ninebot, Xiaomi, Kixin, Okai, Hiboy) account for 80% of global market share. Revenue figures: Xiaomi ($1.2B) and Segway-Ninebot ($1.0B) from company reports and industry estimates.
What this means The electric scooter market is a two-horse race at the top. Xiaomi and Segway-Ninebot together likely exceed 50% share, with Xiaomi leveraging its consumer electronics brand and Segway-Ninebot dominating through shared mobility partnerships (Spin, Bird). For challenger brands, the path to market is through niches — performance enthusiasts, specific price tiers, or regional markets — rather than competing head-on for commuter share.
