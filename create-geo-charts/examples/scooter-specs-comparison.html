Segway Max G3 Delivers 40+ Miles of Real-World Range for Under $1,000

Among the top-selling commuter electric scooters in 2026, the Segway Ninebot Max G3 offers the best range-to-price ratio at 40+ miles for $899. Budget options like the NIU KQi 100F start at $299 but deliver only 11 miles of real-world range. Performance models like the Dualtron Storm hit 70 mph but cost $4,000+. The sweet spot for daily commuters sits in the $500–$900 range with 25–40 miles of real-world range.

Source: ERideHero, 2025 (real-world testing); Electroheads, 2026 (USA buyer's guide); Vepace, 2026. Prices as of March 2026. Real-world range based on independent rider tests at mixed speeds, not manufacturer claims.
What this means For daily commuters, the $500–$900 price tier offers the best value — 25-40 miles of real-world range covers most round-trip urban commutes. Spending over $2,000 gets diminishing returns for commuting (you're paying for speed, not range). Budget scooters under $400 are suitable only for last-mile trips under 5 miles. The Segway Max G3 currently offers the strongest all-around value proposition for commuters.
