Smart AI Toy Market to Reach $25B by 2035, Up From $7.7B in 2024

The global smart AI toy market was valued at $7.69 billion in 2024 and is projected to reach $25 billion by 2035, growing at a CAGR of 11.3%. North America leads with 36.4% market share, while Asia-Pacific is the fastest-growing region at 18.9% CAGR. AI companion robots — the fastest-growing sub-segment — reached $48.9 million in 2024 with a projected 9.1% CAGR.

Source: Market Research Future, 2025. Smart AI Toy Market Size: $7.69B (2024) to $25B (2035). 2028 and 2031 values interpolated at 11.3% CAGR. Regional data from Mordor Intelligence, 2025; companion robot sub-segment from Intel Market Research, 2025.
What this means The AI toy market is growing at double the rate of the traditional toy industry (~4% CAGR). The category is transitioning from novelty to mainstream — driven by parents seeking educational value and children's increasing comfort with conversational AI. However, safety and privacy concerns (see PIRG 2025 report) may slow adoption if not addressed by manufacturers.
